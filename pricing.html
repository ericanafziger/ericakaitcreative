    Weddings

    Engaged? First of all, congratulations! Documenting weddings is something I am very passionate about. I want to help make this time as special for you as possible, and help you to avoid unneccessary stress. I strive to be upfront about my prices and what you will receive for your investment. I have three wedding packages to choose from. These prices are based in the Portland Metro area, but I'd love to talk to you more about travel pricing if you're out of state!

    Classic Package

    Starts at $2000
    • 6 hours of wedding day coverage
    • Timeline consultation
    • At least 500 edited photos in an online gallery

    Deluxe Package

    Starts at $2500
    • 7 hours of wedding day coverage
    • Timeline consultation
    • At least 600 edited photos in an online gallery
    • Complimentary engagement session

    Ultimate Package

    Starts at $3000
    • 8 hours of wedding day coverage
    • Timeline consultation
    • At least 700 edited photos in an online gallery
    • Complimentary engagement session
    • 3-4 hours of second photography coverage

    Additional time can be added on for $200 an hour. We can also discuss more options that include a second photographer on your wedding day. If you are interested in booking me, please contact me by email or phone so we can discuss your wedding more thoroughly in person or over the phone. I'd love to sit down and chat about your expectations and vision for your day!

    Portrait Packages

    I offer all sorts of different portraits sessions including senior photos, family photos, maternity sessions, engagement photos, and branding shoots. Below are the two main packages I offer. If you're interested in something more custom feel free to reach out to me directly and we can chat more about what you have in mind.

    Mini Session

    $250
    • + 45-minute shoot
    • + High resolution digital images
    • + At least 50 edited photos

    Portrait Session

    $350
    • + 1.5 hours of shooting
    • + 2nd location within 15 minutes of the first
    • + 1 to 2 outfit changes
    • + At least 100 edited photos in an online gallery
