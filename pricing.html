    Weddings

    Engaged? First of all, congratulations! Documenting weddings is what I am passionate about. It is such a delight to witness and photograph the celebration of two people committed to spending their lives together. I want to help make this time as special for you as possible, and help you to avoid unneccessary stress. I strive to be upfront about my prices and what you will receive for your investment.

    Weddings in the Portland Metro Area start at $1300. This includes:

    • + A complimentary 1 hour engagement session
    • + 6 hours of wedding coverage
    • + High resolution digital images
    • + At least 300 final edited photos

    From this base package I am always willing to add on additional time for $150 an hour. We can also discuss travel costs for weddings outside of the Portland area, and the cost of including a second photographer. If you are interested in booking me, please contact me by email or phone so we can discuss your wedding more thoroughly in person or over the phone. I'd love to sit down and chat about your expectations and vision for your day.

    After we've talked, I will give you a contract to sign. Once that is returned to me I will put your wedding date into my calendar. Then, once your wedding day is closer, we will work together to make a day of timeline and and list of photos I will make sure to capture!

    Portrait Packages

    Portraits are a great way to remember a significant event or memory in our lives. I offer many different kinds of portraits including senior photos, family photos, maternity sessions, engagement photos, and fashion shoots. Below are the different packages I offer.

    $150 Mini Session includes:

    • + 45 minute shoot
    • + High resolution digital images
    • + At least 25 edited photos

    $200 Standard Session includes:

    • + 90 minute shoot
    • + 2nd location within 15 minutes of the first
    • + 1 to 2 outfit changes
    • + High resolution digital images
    • + At least 50 edited photos

    $250 Ultimate Session includes:

    • + 120 minute shoot
    • + 2nd location within 15 minutes of the first
    • + Multiple outfit changes
    • + High resolution digital images
    • + At least 75 edited photos
