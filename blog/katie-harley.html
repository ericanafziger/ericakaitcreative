    Katie & Harley Portraits

    Eagle, Idaho

    Portrait Session - June 2016

    Having summers off as a teacher is a definite perk. This summer I hung out in Eagle, Idaho to get some much needed sunshine that can't always be guaranteed in Portland. While I was there, I got the chance to hang out with Katie and her sweet baby Harley who had just turned one. We spent some time exploring Eagle Island State Park in the nice warm Idaho weather. Harley was unsure about the whole picture taking thing, but she eventually seemed to warm up to the idea.
