    April + Elijah Newborn Session

    Salem, OR

    Newborn Session - June 2017

    I met April in her home to photograph her and Elijah. This is the tiniest babe I've taken pictures of to date, and I just couldn't get over his little fingers and toes, and his great big yawns. He was happy as a clam the entire session and his face absolutely lit up when his mama was holding him in her arms.


    I love in-home sessions because they allow for such beautiful quiet momories to be captured. It adds an extra personal touch to photos and allows for the most simple, natural moments to be captured. Those natural laughs and smiles are the ones I just can't get enough of!
