    Kortney & Ebony

    Rooster Rock State Park

    Portrait Session - September 2016

    Kortney and I were friends in high school, so reconnecting with her now that we are living in the same city has been lots of fun and full of reminiscing. Taking photos with Kortney (and her puppy Ebony) in the Columbia River Gorge was such a blast. We explored around the area and got out to take some photos, waiting for the sun to set. Then we realized we had explored a little too long and almost missed the sunset altogether! We literally ran down the beach at Rooster Rock hoping to catch some of the last rays and made it just in time. I love how serene these photos look despite the franticness. Lucky for us, the Gorge is beautiful just after sunset too!.
