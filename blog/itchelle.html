    Itchelle

    Cape Kiwanda - Oregon Coast

    Portrait Session - September 2016

    Photographing Itchelle at Cape Kiwanda was a dream. We headed to the beach late in the afternoon looking for some magic, and the Oregon Coast delivered! Balancing on rocks in heels and a dress is no easy feat, but Itchelle pulled it off flawlessly. We both wanted to do darker colors and moodier fall vibes for this shoot, and the outfits Itchelle picked out worked perfectly. This shoot has me dying to go back to Cape Kiwanda sometime soon. Feeling lucky that these views are just a quick drive away.
